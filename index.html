Empanadas

Oven Baked Vegetable Empanada Recipe Images Other Variations

Dough

  • 3 cups flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/3 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 cup hot water
  • 1 egg

Vegetable Filling

  • 5 oz. mushrooms finely diced
  • 1/2 medium onion finely dices
  • 1/2 to 1 carrot shredded
  • 1 cup (approximately) broccoli florets finely chopped
  • 1 package frozen chopped spinach thawed and squeezed of excess waer
  • adobo, salt, pepper, garlic (fresh or dry) to taste

Steps

  1. Make the dough
    1. Put the dry ingredients in a large mixing bowl
    2. Whisk to combine
    3. Add oil and water
    4. Mix to combine until dough forms
    5. Break apart and roll into 2 inch balls
    6. Place one ball at a time between two pieces of parchment or wax paper
    7. Use a heavy pan or tortilla press to flatten each ball to about 1/8 inches thick
  2. Make the filling
    1. Place all ingredients (except spinach) into a pan over medium heat with 2 TBS of olive oil and spices
    2. Cook until softened, about 7 minutes
    3. Add spinach and cook until warmed through
    4. Tast and adjust spices as necessary
  3. Make empanadas
    1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees
    2. Place 1-2 TBS of filling on the lower half of flattened dough leaving about 1/4 inches of edges clear
    3. Dip one clean finger in water and rub over the edges
    4. Fold dough in half over the filling
    5. Press edges together
    6. Use fork tines to crimp edges and seal
    7. Place in parchment lined baking sheet
    8. Repeat until all empanadas are made
    9. Brush egg wash over top of empanadas
    10. Bake for about 18-25 minutes rotating halfway through until golden brown
    11. Let them cool completely before enjoying
